About
A creative mind with an
operational backbone.

I'm Indu — a Social media manager, content strategist, and SEO executive. I bring a rare mix of creative storytelling and operational discipline, shaped by years of managing client accounts, vacation rental platforms, and brand communications across multiple channels.

I started in client management and reservations, which taught me how to communicate, coordinate, and keep people happy under pressure. Now I apply that same discipline to social media — building content systems, running ads, and growing brands with intention and consistency.

I work across Instagram, Meta, and LinkedIn for brands ranging from luxury apparel to schools to digital product businesses. A curious learner at heart — always first in line to try a new tool, test a new format, or jump on a fresh tech drop before it becomes mainstream.
